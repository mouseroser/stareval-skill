# 星鉴报告回执模板

所有星鉴阶段的完成回执都不能只写 `done`。

## Step 2 回执

```text
Title: <title>
Conclusion: <one-paragraph conclusion>
RecommendedRoute: <recommended route>
Risks: <top risks>
ReportPath: <path>
```

## Step 3 回执

```text
Title: <title>
Conclusion: <final conclusion>
RecommendedArchitecture: <architecture placement>
PhasePlan: <phase 0/1/2>
Risks: <top risks>
ReportPath: <path>
```

## Step 4 回执

```text
Title: <title>
Verdict: ALIGN | DRIFT | MAJOR_DRIFT
Why: <reason>
FixSuggestion: <how to fix if needed>
ReportPath: <path>
```

## Step 5 回执

```text
Title: <title>
Verdict: GO | REVISE | BLOCK
Rationale: <reason>
KeyRisk: <top risk>
ReportPath: <path>
```

## Step 6 回执

```text
Title: <title>
FinalSummary: <final delivery summary>
Recommendation: <recommended next action>
Risks: <remaining risks>
ReportPath: <path>
```
